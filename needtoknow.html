Line of Authority

  • Ops Spec is created by an airline
  • Draft of Ops Spec is given to FAA
  • FAA reads airline's Ops Spec
  • FAA approves airline's Ops Spec

Duty Times

Dispatcher Duty Times (FAR 121.465)

  • 10 hours scheduled/shift
  • 8 hours rest between shifts
  • 1 day off per week (24 hours every 7 days)
  • 5 hours of jumpseating per year +1 hour per landing (up to half of requirement)

Pilot Duty Times

  • 190 Flight duty period per 672 hours
  • 14 Max hours in short-call reserve
  • 2 Hours flight duty period can be extended due to unforeseen circumstances (on the ground) (in the air, as long as it takes)

Fatigue Rules

  • Chronic Fatigue: Not enough rest day after day
    • Solution: Several nights of extended rest
  • Acute Fatigue: Not enough rest over one night
    • Solution: One night of extended rest

Three Required Documents to Make a Flight Legal (FAR 121.695)

  • Dispatch Release (FATWIVES)
    • Fuel (Minimum Supply)
    • Airports
    • Trip or Tail #
    • Weather Package
    • IFR (Instrument Flight Rules)
    • VFR (Visual Flight Rules)
    • ETOPS/ETP
    • Signatures (PIC & DX)
  • Load Manifest
    • AC Weight
    • Fuel
    • Cargo
    • Crewmembers
    • Passengers
  • Flight Plan
    • Route of flight: e.g., KSAN.SID.DCT.WAYPT.DCT.STAR.KDAL
